How it Works

One-Click Access

To open OnlineBrowserTools, click or hover over the icon on the right side of your browser.

Select Your Tool

A side menu with a selection of tools will appear. Click the option you’d like to use – that's it!

Full App Control

Click settings within the side menu to view and adjust your preferences.

GETTING STARTED

Click ‘Add Extension’

Kick off installation by clicking the orange ‘Add Extension’ button.

Add to Chrome

Once in the Chrome Web Store, click ‘Add to Chrome’ in the upper right corner.

Confirm Installation

Click ‘Add to Chrome to confirm installing the extension.

That’s it!

Once installation is complete, you can start using OnlineBrowserTools right away!
